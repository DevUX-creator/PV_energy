## What's common across Trafigura + BB Energy (and how we use it)

These themes appear on **both** competitor sites and are the backbone of every
subpage below. PV Link should say the same things, in its own voice, at its own
scale.

| Common theme | How competitors frame it | PV Link angle |
|---|---|---|
| **Connecting producers & consumers** | "bring resilience and trust to complex supply chains"; "connecting producers and consumers" | "Empowering Global Energy Flows" — we connect supply and demand across the global energy market |
| **End-to-end value chain** | trading · logistics/shipping · storage/infrastructure · financing · risk | our exact 6 services: Trading · Supply & Distribution · Storage & Blending · Shipping & Chartering · Hedging & Risk · Financial Solutions |
| **Global footprint + local execution** | "50+ locations", "150+ countries"; BB across "7+ countries" | 20+ countries, 5 strategic hubs (Middle East, Asia-Pacific, Europe/ARA, West Africa, the Americas) |
| **Breadth of products** | oil & refined products, LPG, fuel oil, distillates, base oils, bitumen | Petroleum line **+** a dedicated **Fertilizer** department (our differentiator) |
| **Responsibility / safety / compliance** | ESG pillars, decarbonization, "responsible sourcing" | responsible energy practices, ISO-compliant operations, rigorous QA |
| **Trust & long-term partnerships** | "trust", "responsible performance and long-term value" | "we believe in long-term partnerships, not one-off trades" |
| **Expertise & market intelligence** | "market expertise", analytics, price discovery | "expertise-driven, data-backed decisions" |

**Where PV Link differs (lean into these):** agricultural commodities + a real
Fertilizer department alongside petroleum; a lean, relationship-led operator
(not an asset-heavy major) — agile, transparent, client-first.
