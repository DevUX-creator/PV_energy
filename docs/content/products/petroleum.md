## Base Oil

**Tagline:** The Foundation of Quality Lubricants · **Origin:** Global

Group I, II, and III base oils — the foundation of finished lubricants — supplied
to blenders and manufacturers with consistent quality and reliable availability.

- **Group I** — solvent-refined conventional base stocks
- **Group II** — hydrotreated stocks for higher-performance lubricants
- **Group III** — high-viscosity-index stocks for premium and synthetic blends
- **Supply to blending** — dependable volumes for lubricant manufacturers
