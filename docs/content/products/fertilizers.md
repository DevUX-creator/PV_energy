## Urea

**Tagline:** Concentrated Nitrogen for High-Yield Crops

The world's most widely used nitrogen fertilizer (46% N), supplied in granular
and prilled forms for broad-acre and specialty agriculture.

- **Forms** — granular and prilled
- **Agricultural use** — high-yield nitrogen for major crops
- **Technical use** — industrial and feed applications
- **Handling** — bulk and bagged supply with moisture control

## Ammonia

**Tagline:** High-Purity Feedstock

Anhydrous ammonia for fertilizer production and industrial use — a core building
block for the nitrogen value chain.

- **Feedstock** — upstream input for nitrogen fertilizers
- **Industrial** — refrigeration, chemicals, and emissions control
- **Handling** — specialized pressurized/refrigerated logistics

## NPK Blends

**Tagline:** Balanced Nutrition, Customizable Ratios

Multi-nutrient blends combining nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium in
customizable ratios for crop- and soil-specific programs.

- **Custom ratios** — formulations matched to agronomy
- **Crop-specific** — programs by crop and growth stage
- **Bulk blending & supply** — flexible volumes to distributors

## DAP (Diammonium Phosphate)

**Tagline:** Phosphorus-Rich Crop Nutrition

A leading phosphate fertilizer supplying concentrated phosphorus and nitrogen
for strong root development and early growth.

- **Nutrition** — high phosphorus plus nitrogen
- **Crops** — broad-acre and high-demand phosphate crops
- **Bulk supply** — reliable volumes to agricultural markets

## Ammonium Sulfate

**Tagline:** Nitrogen + Sulfur for Alkaline Soils

A dual-nutrient fertilizer delivering nitrogen and sulfur — especially valuable
for alkaline soils and sulfur-deficient crops.

- **Dual nutrient** — nitrogen and sulfur in one product
- **Alkaline soils** — lowers pH while feeding crops
- **Forms** — granular and crystalline supply
