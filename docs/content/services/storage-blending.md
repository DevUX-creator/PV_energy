## Overview

Strategic storage and precision blending let us hold, condition, and tailor
products to the exact grades our partners need — decoupling supply timing from
demand and unlocking value between markets and specifications.

## What we do

- Access **strategic storage** at key hubs to balance supply and demand
- **Blend to specification** — fuel grades, sulfur content, and quality targets
- Hold inventory to capture **timing and arbitrage** opportunities
- Guarantee **quality assurance and testing** before every release

## Why it matters

Storage is optionality; blending is precision. Together they let us deliver the
exact product a buyer needs, when they need it — rather than whatever the spot
market happens to offer that day.

## Capabilities

- Tank storage access across strategic locations
- In-tank and in-line blending to target specifications
- Independent quality testing and certification
- Inventory and demurrage management
