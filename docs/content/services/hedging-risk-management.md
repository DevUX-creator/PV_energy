## Overview

We manage price and market exposure with disciplined hedging — protecting every
trade against volatility so our partners can plan with confidence. Risk
management is built into how we trade, not bolted on afterward.

## What we do

- **Hedge price exposure** across physical positions using paper instruments
- Monitor **market, credit, and operational risk** in real time
- Structure trades to **lock in margins** and absorb market swings
- Provide partners with **transparency** on the risk behind every deal

## Why it matters

Commodity prices can move sharply between a deal being struck and a cargo being
delivered. Our hedging discipline turns that uncertainty into stability — for
our book and for the customers who rely on predictable pricing.

## Capabilities

- Futures, swaps, and options hedging
- Mark-to-market and exposure monitoring
- Credit and counterparty risk control
- Scenario analysis and stress testing
