## Overview

Trade finance and structured solutions keep deals liquid, bankable, and moving —
backed by strong financial expertise and banking relationships. We make the
capital side of a trade as reliable as the physical side.

## What we do

- Arrange **trade finance** — letters of credit, guarantees, and working capital
- Structure deals to be **bankable and well-collateralized**
- Bridge **financing to purchasing agreements** so supply can scale
- Manage **payment, settlement, and FX** across counterparties and currencies

## Why it matters

Many good trades stall on financing, not fundamentals. By bringing financial
structuring in-house, we unlock deals that would otherwise be impossible — and
give partners confidence that capital will never be the bottleneck.

## Capabilities

- Letters of credit and bank guarantees
- Structured and pre-export trade finance
- Working-capital and settlement solutions
- FX and payment management
