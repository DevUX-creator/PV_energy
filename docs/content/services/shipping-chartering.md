## Overview

We charter and manage vessels across key trade routes, handling marine logistics
end-to-end so cargo moves safely, efficiently, and on time. From fixture to
discharge, we own the freight so our partners don't have to.

## What we do

- **Charter and manage tonnage** matched to cargo, route, and laycan
- Optimize **routing and scheduling** across global trade lanes
- Manage **port operations, demurrage, and documentation**
- Uphold **maritime safety and compliance** at every stage

## Why it matters

Freight is one of the largest variables in any physical trade. Controlling it
directly — rather than relying on third parties — lets us protect both the
schedule and the economics of every cargo.

## Capabilities

- Spot and term chartering
- Voyage planning and route optimization
- Port agency, demurrage, and laytime management
- Marine safety, vetting, and compliance
