## Overview

We keep product moving. From the moment a cargo is sourced to the moment it is
delivered, we coordinate volumes, terminals, and transport so that deliveries
arrive on-specification and on schedule — across borders, modes, and markets.

## What we do

- Plan and coordinate **supply programs** against demand across our hubs
- Manage **terminal access, scheduling, and inventory** along the route
- Orchestrate **multimodal transport** — sea, road, rail, and pipeline
- Maintain **quality and compliance** at every transfer point

## Why it matters

Distribution is where trades succeed or fail. A single missed window, terminal
slot, or quality check can strand a cargo. We own that operational risk so our
partners get a clean, predictable handover at destination.

## Capabilities

- End-to-end supply-chain coordination
- Terminal and inventory management
- Customs, documentation, and regulatory compliance
- Last-mile delivery into industrial, shipping, and downstream markets
